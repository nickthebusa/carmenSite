Welcome

For well over a decade Mobile Phlebotomy and Wellness has been in the health and wellness field and serve many surrounding communities. We serve areas of Plymouth County, Bristol County, Providence County, and Barnstable County. We bring a full-service, comprehensive approach to your health needs.

In the growing industry of specialty labs conducting specialized blood tests, we have expanded to come to you and conduct your blood draw in the convenience of your own home! We have flexible hours for your busy schedule. We handle everything from start to finish - the lab requisition, interpreting instructions from your lab kit, drawing your blood, packing it up, processing everything including shipping it out for you!

Why do we do what we do? We want you to feel better, know your health status and get your lab results as soon as possible so your provider can get you on the proper course to feeling better!
