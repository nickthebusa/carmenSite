Mobile Phlebotomy

As the demand for specialized blood testing continues to grow, we’ve expanded our services to meet you where you are—right in the comfort of your own home. With flexible hours to accommodate your busy schedule, we make the process simple and stress-free.

From beginning to end, we take care of everything:

  • reviewing your lab requisition
  • following your lab kit’s instructions
  • drawing and processing your blood
  • securely packaging the specimen
  • shipping it out for you

Our thorough handling ensures your sample reaches the lab safely and on time, so your results are delivered promptly and your provider can guide you toward the right treatment plan without delay.
